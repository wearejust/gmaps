Overlay

Use the content of the li's as Overlays. Overlays are custom Info Windows.

  • Just

    Uses data-gmaps-location and title attributes.

  • Burcht

    Uses separate data-gmaps-lat and data-gmaps-lng and data-gmaps-title attributes.

  • LUMC

    Uses separate data-gmaps-latitude and data-gmaps-longitude attributes and gmaps-title class on the header.
