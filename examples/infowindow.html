Info Window

Use the content of the li's as Info Windows. Uses different ways to position and display markers. Hover over them to display its title.

Supports default and specific options, like maxWidth.

Use [tab] key to run through, [space] or [enter] to open and [esc] to close.

  • Just

    Uses data-gmaps-location and title attributes.

  • Burcht

    Uses separate data-gmaps-lat and data-gmaps-lng and data-gmaps-title attributes.

  • LUMC

    Uses separate data-gmaps-latitude and data-gmaps-longitude attributes and gmaps-title class on the header.
